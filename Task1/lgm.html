About Us

LetsGrowMore is a community of the students, for the students and by the students.

Goal

Our goal is to help others to gain personal and professional skills in the area of Technological development. The purpose is to provide a structure through which students may exercise their right to freely associate in pursuit of a common purpose or goal that enhances community at LetsGrowMore.

Technology

Technology that we are currently using, Software that we use on a daily basis all come from the term ‘Open Source Development’ and are called ‘Open Source Software’. Open source is a term that originally referred to open source software (OSS). Open-source software means that its source code is designed to be publicly accessible by anyone and anyone can modify it and contribute to its source code .
